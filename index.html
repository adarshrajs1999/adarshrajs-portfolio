Discover the Divine Story of Radha and Krishna

Introduction

ISKCON spreads the teachings of Lord Krishna, highlighting the eternal love between Radha and Krishna, a symbol of spiritual devotion and purity.

Radha & Krishna Story

Their divine union represents the soul's longing for God. The rasa lila and pastimes show the joy of spiritual connection and unconditional love.

Significance in ISKCON

Festivals like Janmashtami and Radhastami celebrate Radha-Krishna through music, dance, and devotion across ISKCON temples worldwide.
